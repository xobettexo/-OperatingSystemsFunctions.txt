Imagen your computer operating system as the boss of a large department store. The entire store is run by a team of specialized managers. Each manager has a certain area of expertise and is responsible for all functions and operations regarding their specific department. In this “store” there is a memory manager, file manager, processor manager and device manager. 
The operating system or the “boss” of the department store. The boss’s job is to control all of the departments aka all hardware and all software. The operating system is responsible for making sure all commands from cooperate (the computer system) is met and if not make the arrangements for notifying the consumer (error message).
